NoMSG $|$ No More Simple Groups

Never used NoMSG before?

No problem. It's super easy. See where it says "Pick a number...?" Type any number in that box and click "Go!" NoMSG will either spit out a proof that no simple groups of that order exist, or it will find a simple group of that order.

In rare cases, it will fail to find a satisfactory proof, even though it knows no simple groups of that order exist. In that case, it will tell you so, and then give you as much information as it can about the what a simple group of that order would have to look like, where one to exist.
