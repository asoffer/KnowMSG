NoMSG $|$ No More Simple Groups

KnowMSG is the ideal tool to field your group theory questions!

It's really simple. Pick your favorite positive integer, type it in where it says "Pick a number..." and click "Go!" KnowMSG will either

  • spit out a proof that no simple groups of that order exist, or

  • find a simple group of that order.

Simple groups are at the core of understanding the content of any algebra course. I hope KnowMSG supports your understanding of this central subject in mathematics.
